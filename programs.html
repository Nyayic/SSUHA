Support to Primary Health Care services

SSUHA’s establishment was on background of provision of both primary and secondary health programs. In general, SSUHA supports the provision of Basic Primary Health Care services at static health facilities as well as mobile outreaches in the four counties of Yei River, Lainya, Morobo and Kajo keji in addition to responding to COVID 19 pandemic, with funding from Health Pooled Fund (HPF).

COVID 19 response are both at health facilities and community levels as follows:

Working at Health facilities level conducting screening for COVID 19 as well as vaccination, training of health workers on how to detect and identify COVID-19 cases, training of health workers on rational and correct and appropriate use of PPEs and the guidelines on its prevention, and also the rational use of other barriers (gloves, masks, aprons, face shields).

training on how to maintain health workers safety and limit the spread of COVID-19 within the health facilities and the community, and strengthening infection control services at health facilities with focus on hand washing (water buckets, soap, hand sanitizers), provision of PPEs (masks, gloves, alcohol based sanitizers and aprons).

Working at the level of communities through support to Boma Health workers/ Community health promoters to provide awareness raising in communities on COVID 19 prevention measures such as use of clothed face masks, hand washing and social distancing, COVID-19 vaccination at community level, training of Boma Health Workers/ community health promoters on home management of flue like symptoms and health education on behavior change.

Settling Refugees in IDP Camps

Health

Coordinate, collaborate and partner with the Ministry of Health (MoH), LGAs and other stakeholders to support health services.

Water & Sanitation

Promote health and hygiene awareness, form and train community -based WASH committees, provide spare parts and tools for rehabilitation of water sources, conduct quality testing to provide safe water, facilitate and promote establishment of water sources, public VIP latrines and household pit latrines.

Sexual & Reporductive Health

Provide ANC and safe motherhood, promote family planning, promote child health, promote adolescent reproductive health, manage abortions, HIV/AIDS/STIs, promote management and prevention of infertility

HIV & AIDS

Promote VCT and access to ARVs, prevent MTCT, promote home-based care, campaign against co-infections, promote the use of condoms, promote community and stakeholders’ engagement in campaign, promote the use of IEC materials and radio talk shows

Food security & Livelihoods

Increase and diversify agricultural production, facilitate surplus production, improve natural resource management and sustainable land use option.

Protection

Provide psychosocial support, conduct case management, strengthen community linkages and referral pathways for SGBV, provide women and girls/boys friendly spaces, conduct family tracing and re-unification, provide training and awareness at schools and host communities.
